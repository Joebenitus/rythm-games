Why I love rythm games

What is a rythm game?

A rythm game is any video game that requires the player to perform high-precision tasks to the beat of music. Rythm games you may have heard of include Guitar Hero, Rockband, and Dance Dance Revolution.

Reasons I love rythm games:

  • Impressive to watch
  • Great at improving hand-eye coordination
  • I have always loved music, which makes it that much more enjoyable
  • Improves reaction time

This is why I love rythm games!
